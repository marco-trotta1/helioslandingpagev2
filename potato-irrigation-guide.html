Potatoes leave little room for guesswork

When the field is moving toward stress, timing matters. A calendar can help organize the season, but it cannot tell you what soil moisture and weather are doing right now.

Read the signals before you apply water

Soil moisture, recent weather, and crop water demand each add part of the picture. Reviewing them together helps you make the next pass with a clearer reason behind it.
