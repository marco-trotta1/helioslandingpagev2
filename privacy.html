Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

This policy explains how Irrigant handles information submitted through the Helios waitlist.

Information we collect

When you join the waitlist, we collect your first name, last name, email address, farm-size range, and location.

How we use and share it

We use this information to manage the waitlist and communicate with you about Helios. Formspree processes waitlist submissions for us. We use Google Fonts to display this website. We do not sell your personal information or share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

Your choices

You may ask to access, correct, delete, or stop receiving communications about your information by emailing marcotrotta909@gmail.com. We will handle applicable privacy requests in accordance with law.

Retention and security

We retain waitlist information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes above or to meet legal obligations. We use reasonable safeguards, but no internet transmission or storage system is completely secure.

Children and changes

This site is not directed to children under 13. We may update this policy when our practices change and will post the revised effective date here.
