Better information before the water decision

Helios is Irrigant’s decision-support tool for growers who want a clearer read before they decide whether to irrigate, when to run, and how much water to apply.

Join the waitlist →

Start with the conditions in front of you

Helios brings together soil moisture, crop context, near-term weather, and crop water demand. The goal is simple: make the conditions around the field easier to read.

See what is driving the call

A good recommendation should not feel like a black box. Helios is built to show the factors behind the next call, so the grower can see the reason as well as the recommendation.

Your judgment stays in control

Helios supports the grower’s decision. It does not operate irrigation equipment or replace local knowledge. Pilot farms get early access while the product is built around real operating decisions.
