How to make a better irrigation call

Irrigation scheduling is not about following a date on the calendar. It is about seeing what the field needs before the decision becomes urgent.

Join the waitlist →

The decision is bigger than a date on the calendar

A better call starts with what is happening in the field now, what weather is coming next, and how quickly your crop is using water. Put those signals together and the next move becomes easier to see.

What to watch before you irrigate

Start with soil moisture. Then look at the forecast, recent rainfall, and crop water demand. None of these tells the whole story alone. Together, they give you the context behind the next irrigation decision.
