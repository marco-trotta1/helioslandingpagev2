What Helios Does

A cloud-based irrigation decision-support system built for real farms — not a black box, not automation, just better information at the right time.

🌱

Soil Moisture Prediction

XGBoost and LightGBM models trained on local field data forecast your soil moisture 24–72 hours into the future, accounting for weather, soil type, and crop stage.

📊

Confidence Scoring

Every recommendation comes with a confidence interval so you know how certain the model is. No hidden decisions — you always see the reasoning.

💧

Water / Wait Recommendations

A single, clear output: irrigate now, or hold. Designed for the way farmers actually make decisions — not to replace your judgment, but to sharpen it.

☁️

Cloud-Based Access

Access Helios from any device with a browser. No hardware to install, no software to maintain. Your data stays secure and available anywhere you work.

🔍

Feature Importance

See which variables — temperature, recent rainfall, root-zone conditions — are driving each prediction. Full transparency into the model's logic.

🏗️

Decision Support, Not Automation

Helios is built as a decision tool. You're the expert on your farm — we just give you better data to work with. No automated valves, no surprises.
